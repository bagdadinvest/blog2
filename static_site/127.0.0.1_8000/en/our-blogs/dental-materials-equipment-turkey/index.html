Are Turkish Dental Materials and Equipment Up to International Standards?

/ Oct. 3, 2024 / Branding

High-Quality Materials and Equipment

The materials used in Turkish dental clinics are sourced from globally recognized manufacturers. Brands like 3M, Nobel Biocare, and Straumann provide dental implants, veneers, and crowns, ensuring long-lasting results.

1. International Certifications

Many dental materials used in Turkey come with CE certifications, which are recognized throughout the European Union as a mark of safety and quality. This includes dental implants, orthodontic materials, and cosmetic dental tools.

2. Cutting-Edge Technology

Turkish clinics invest in advanced dental technologies like 3D imaging, CAD/CAM technology for custom crowns, and laser dentistry tools for precision treatments.

How Clinics Stay Up-to-Date

Many clinics regularly update their equipment to meet international standards. They follow advancements in dental care closely and bring in the latest technologies to improve treatment outcomes.

3. Minimally Invasive Technologies

Advanced tools like digital scanners, laser technology, and minimally invasive techniques are common in Turkish clinics, ensuring patients experience shorter recovery times and more comfortable procedures.
