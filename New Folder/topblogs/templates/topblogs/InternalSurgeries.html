{% trans "Internal surgeries, also known as invasive surgeries, involve procedures that require an incision or entry into the body." %}

{% trans "In Turkey, internal surgeries are performed using advanced medical technologies and techniques. The country has a strong healthcare system with well-trained surgeons and modern facilities." %}

{% trans "Common types of internal surgeries performed in Turkey include cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, and orthopedic surgeries." %}

{% trans "Cardiovascular surgeries in Turkey include coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair, and aortic aneurysm repair." %}

{% trans "Gastrointestinal surgeries address issues related to the digestive system, such as gastric bypass, colectomy, and liver resection." %}

{% trans "Orthopedic surgeries focus on the musculoskeletal system, including joint replacements and spine surgeries." %}

{% trans "Cardiovascular Surgeries" %}

{% trans "Turkey is renowned for its expertise in cardiovascular surgeries, utilizing minimally invasive techniques for better recovery outcomes." %}

{% trans "Gastrointestinal Surgeries" %}

{% trans "Gastrointestinal surgeries in Turkey are performed by skilled surgeons specializing in digestive health, using laparoscopic techniques." %}

{% trans "Orthopedic Surgeries" %}

{% trans "Orthopedic surgeries in Turkey include joint replacements, spinal fusions, and fracture repairs with advanced imaging and surgical technology." %}

{% trans "High Standards of Care" %}

{% trans "Turkey's hospitals are accredited by international organizations, ensuring stringent quality and safety standards." %}

{% trans "In conclusion, Turkey is a leading destination for internal surgeries, offering advanced medical treatments at competitive prices." %}
